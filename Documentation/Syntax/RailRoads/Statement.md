
# Statement

A **Statement** is a single instruction that does
not explicitly evaluate as **Expression**.
A *Declaration* is also considered a **Statement**.

### Syntax:

    + ─╮── > ── [Block Statement] ────────── > ──╭─ +
       ╰── > ── [Break Statement] ────────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Continue Statement] ─────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Delete Statement] ───────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Do While Statement] ─────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Expression Statement] ───── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [For Statement] ──────────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [If Statement] ───────────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Import Statement] ───────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Print Statement] ────────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Repeat Until Statement] ─── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Rest Statement] ─────────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Return Statement] ───────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [Until Statement] ────────── > ──╯
       ╰── > ── [While Statement] ────────── > ──╯
